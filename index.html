About Me

I love the coastal town of Mombasa, which is where I work and live with my family. I grew up watching cartoons like the Roadrunner show, Popeye the sailor and my most favorite Tom and Jerry. I have been to Mt. Kenya and got mountain sick with just 4 kilometers to the peak. I started my professional career as a teacher in a vocational technical training institute, teaching computer subjects like programming in C++, PASCAL, Data Communication and MySQL databases to college students. I started my Cisco career in 2011 and certified for CCNA in September 2015, in 2019 July I completed my CCNP and that was how my career changed. To simulate networks, I use EVE-NG which is a pretty awesome tool to build network topologies and test multivendor technologies.
