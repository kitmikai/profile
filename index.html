A Journey into Networks and DevOps

Love what you do, and do what you love

I love the coastal town of Mombasa, which is where I work and live with my lovely wife and two kids. I grew up watching cartoons like the Roadrunner show, Popeye the sailor and my most favorite Tom and Jerry. I have watched all Transformers movies and my favourite character is Bumblebee. I climbed Mt. Kenya once and got mountain sick with just 4 kilometers to the peak. I started my professional career as a teacher in a vocational technical training institute, teaching computer subjects like programming in C++, PASCAL, Data Communication and MySQL databases to college students. I started my Cisco career in 2011 and certified for CCNA in September 2015, in 2019 July I completed my CCNP and that was how my career changed. To simulate networks, I use eve-ng which is a pretty awesome tool to build network topologies and test multivendor technologies. Basketball is my favorite game since highschool and my favorite NBA players are Michael Jordan, Kobe Bryant and Allen Iverson.
